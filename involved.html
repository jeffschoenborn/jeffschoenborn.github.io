What Is the Republican Central Committee?

The Maryland Republican Central Committee is the official governing body of the Republican Party in each jurisdiction. It fills vacancies in elected offices, supports Republican candidates, manages party operations, and serves as the voice of Republican voters in Baltimore City. Your vote in this election directly shapes the direction of the local party.
